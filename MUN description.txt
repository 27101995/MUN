The name of the project is Municipal utility network. Basically its a complaint based system designed for the municipality corporation of city. It has three modules first one is client side module from which any resident can complaint about any issue pertaining cleanliness of their locality online through this application by providing his basic details. Second module is local head, when the client files any complaint it is verified in the server from government website that the resident is authentic or not after that the complaint is forwarded to his local area head as well as to the main municipal head office local head can alter the status of the complaint. And the third module is main municipal officer who is monitoring the whole process. And the interesting part is the client gets the unique complaint code after successful submission of the complaint in order to check the status of his complaint.
